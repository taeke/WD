Each turn a [!:IPlayer] goes through a couple of fases. This enum defines these fases.

Members

Member nameValueDescription
None0 Before a Call to StartGame there is no real GameFase.
PlaceInitialArmies1 When the [!:IGame] is started the first turn the [!:IPlayer] must place armies on all of his Country.
ChooseTurnType2 The [!:IPlayer] has to decide which TurnType he wants to play this time. He can choose this only once during each turn.
ExchangeCards3 The [!:IPlayer] may choose to Exchanges CardType for new Armies. Every turn every [!:IPlayer] goes through this fase even if he doesn't own a combination of CardType he can exchanges.
PlaceNewArmies4 The [!:IPlayer] has got new Armies and is in the process of placing them. He must place all his new Armies before he can go to the next fase. Which GameFase that is depents on the TurnType.
Attack5 The [!:IPlayer] has decided he wants to attack. He can get in this fase multiple times during 1 turn and will go to MoveArmiesAfterAttack if the attack was succesfull or stays in this fase if the attack was not succesfull. He can decide to place another attack or go to the MoveArmiesEndOfTurn fase.
MoveArmiesAfterAttack6 The [!:IPlayer] has succesfully attacked a Country and has to decide how many armies he wants to Move to the new Country. He can get in this fase multiple times during one turn.
MoveArmiesEndOfTurn7 The [!:IPlayer] has indicated he wants to end his attacks and still may move armies from one of his Country to another.
